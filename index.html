Help
Quick answers.
When do I get paid?
GIC pays automatically when two conditions are both true: a real disruption in your zone AND your activity has dropped below your personal baseline. You never need to file anything. Payment arrives within minutes.
Why didn't my claim fire?
Either the external trigger didn't cross its threshold (e.g., rain was below 15mm/hr), or your activity drop wasn't significant enough. Both conditions must be true simultaneously.
How is my premium calculated?
Your premium recalculates every Sunday based on your zone's risk history, your personal claim history, the forecast for next week, and your activity tier. Floor: ₹39. Ceiling: ₹89.
What is AI-3?
AI-3 compares your activity against every other worker in your zone. If the whole zone went quiet because of rain, your claim is genuine. If only you stopped working while everyone else kept going, that's a flag.
Is my location tracked?
Location is used only to confirm your active zone, which determines what triggers apply to you. It is never stored or shared. You can always manually select your zone instead.
